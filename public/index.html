Welcome to SmartLocket

SmartLocket is an NFC-powered keepsake that lets you save and relive your most meaningful moments with just one tap. Instead of endless photos, SmartLocket focuses on what truly matters — a small, intentional collection of 3–5 memories that define your story. Tap your SmartLocket and instantly open a private memory page in your browser. No apps. No clutter. Just the moments you chose.

SmartLocket Features

  • One-Tap NFC Access Instantly open your memory page by tapping SmartLocket with your phone.
  • Curated Memories (3–5 Photos) Store only your most meaningful moments — no clutter, just what matters.
  • Viewable by Anyone, Editable by You Anyone can scan and view; edits stay private and secure.
  • No App Required Works directly in the browser on iPhone and Android.
  • Physical Keepsake + Digital Memory A meaningful object paired with a lasting digital memory.

Get Started

Activate Your SmartLocket Visit the link included in your package or tap your SmartLocket. Add Your Memories Upload up to 3–5 photos that matter most. Lock It In Save your page and your SmartLocket is ready to share.
